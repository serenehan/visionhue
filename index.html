WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Colorblind people often struggle with daily tasks — from choosing clothes, identifying signs, reading colored charts, to even recognizing safety signals like traffic lights. This smart glasses device gives them independence by helping them recognize colors in real time — clearly, simply, and privately.

BENEFIT TO SOCIETY,

  • - helps colorblind students learn better in class (especially in subjects like science or arts).
  • - helps in job settings where color recognition matters (like wiring, designing, or even medicine).
  • - helps users feel included and confident — no more asking others what color something is.
  • - can even help visually impaired users with low vision by giving audio cues for color-based surroundings.
